Hampi

Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage site, is a captivating ancient city in Karnataka, known for its stunning ruins and vibrant history. Once the capital of the Vijayanagara Empire, it boasts majestic temples like Virupaksha and Vittala, along with architectural marvels such as the Stone Chariot. The landscape is dotted with boulders, ruined palaces, and intricate carvings. Hampi's beauty is a blend of spirituality, art, and historical significance, making it a must-visit for history and culture enthusiasts.

Location: Hampi, Karnataka

Stone Chariot

I recreated the iconic Stone Chariot in charcoal while surrounded by Hampi’s spiritual silence. My video shows the process unfold, blending motion, memory, and ancient art into one living canvas.

Location: Hampi, Karnataka

Coorg Monastery

Namdroling Monastery, also known as the Golden Temple, is a Tibetan Buddhist center in Coorg, known for its stunning architecture, vibrant murals, and three large golden Buddha statues.

Location: Coorg, Karnataka

Adiyogi

At the Adiyogi statue in Chikkamagaluru, I found myself captivated by the peaceful yet powerful presence of the towering figure. Its serene expression inspired me to translate its spiritual depth onto paper with charcoal. As I sketched, nature's quiet beauty surrounded me — the cool breeze, the distant sounds of the forest, and the ever-changing light. The video I created captures this profound connection, blending the statue’s stillness with the dynamic energy of the environment, revealing how art and spirituality merged into one harmonious experience

Location: Chikkamagaluru, Karnataka
